Users of Letter Rise may initiate an account deletion / data deletion request either through the App or through the contact channel provided on this page.

1. In-App Deletion

If you can still access the App, you may initiate your deletion request from within the App by following these steps:

  1. Open the Letter Rise App.
  2. Go to the Settings screen.
  3. Tap the Delete Account button.
  4. Complete the confirmation steps shown in the App.

2. If You Cannot Access the App

If you cannot access the App, have deleted it, changed devices, or otherwise cannot reach the in-app deletion option, you may submit your request by email:

Send Account Deletion Request

When contacting us, please include, where possible:

  • App name: Letter Rise
  • Your nickname / username, if any
  • A short statement that your request concerns account deletion / data deletion
  • Any information that may help us identify your account or records

3. Important Information About the Account Structure

Letter Rise may not operate on a traditional email-based or phone-based account model. In some cases, gameplay records may be associated with a device / installation identifier or similar technical identifier.

Because of this structure, if the App has been deleted, the device has been replaced, reset, or lost, or if the relevant records can no longer be matched technically, recovery of the previous account or progress may not be possible.

4. What Happens After a Request

Your request will be reviewed within a reasonable period and handled in accordance with applicable law and technical feasibility.

Deleted accounts or records may not be recoverable.

However, certain records may continue to be retained, to the extent permitted or required by applicable law, for purposes such as fraud prevention, security processes, legal compliance, dispute resolution, or evidentiary obligations. Where appropriate, such data may be deleted, anonymized, or retained only for limited purposes.

5. Withdrawal of Consent

Where processing activities are based on consent, use of the in-app Delete Account feature and/or submission of a deletion request may also function as a withdrawal of consent for relevant consent-based processing activities, to the extent applicable.

Withdrawal of consent applies going forward and does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before the withdrawal.

If consent is withdrawn, some online features or consent-dependent parts of the App may become partially or entirely unavailable.
